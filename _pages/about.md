I'm a backend engineer with a strong enthusiasm for AI and emerging technologies. I enjoy working at the intersection of software engineering and artificial intelligence, applying cutting-edge technologies to solve real-world problems.

Currently working at ING Netherlands, I have experience in developing robust backend systems while staying up-to-date with the latest developments in machine learning and deep learning. My academic background includes a Master's degree from Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, where I published research papers in AI and related fields.

My research interests include:
- Neural Networks and Deep Learning
- Evolutionary Computation
- Multiclass Classification
- Knowledge Representation and Semantic Web

When I'm not coding or doing research, I'm actively involved in the community as a project manager at the Vereniging van Chinese Wetenschappers en Ingenieurs in Nederland (VCWI), where I help organize events and facilitate knowledge sharing among Chinese engineers and scholars in the Netherlands.

You can find my publications on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=F8bdTwUAAAAJ&hl=en), my projects on [GitHub](https://github.com/lafengxiaoyu), and my technical blog on [CSDN](https://blog.csdn.net/lafengxiaoyu).
